A Three.js - CSS3d showcase for an image gallery...

Here you see an image gallery based on css3d. The photos you see are from the default assets/galleries folder.
On the top left within the canvas you can choose a folder with images to load from. (Note: In some browsers the webkitDirectory picker may not work :/ .)
On the top right you can choose the presentation layout of the image gallery.
